# ORPHEA ARCHIVE Production Bible

## Part I — Brand Identity & Art Direction

**Version 1.0 Draft**

---

# 1. Brand Philosophy

## Vision

**Orphea Archive** is not a historical encyclopedia.

It is an interactive archive that asks the player to survive history rather than simply observe it.

History is experienced through ordinary people, not heroes.

The player is never asked:

> *"Do you know history?"*

Instead, the Archive asks:

> *"Would you survive it?"*

---

## Mission

Transform historical events into emotional simulations.

Players should leave each episode understanding not only **what happened**, but **why ordinary people made impossible choices.**

---

## Core Experience

The player enters history as someone with almost no power.

Victory is never guaranteed.

Correct choices may still lead to tragedy.

History is not fair.

---

# 2. Core Keywords

Every visual and narrative decision must reinforce these ideas.

| Keyword     | Meaning                                            |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Memory      | History is preserved memory.                       |
| Choice      | Every episode revolves around difficult decisions. |
| Observation | The Archive observes rather than judges.           |
| Consequence | Every action has historical weight.                |
| Humanity    | Ordinary people are the protagonists.              |
| Truth       | Historical truth is often uncomfortable.           |

---

# 3. Creative Pillars

Every new episode should satisfy all five pillars.

---

## Pillar 1

History before Fantasy.

Fantasy exists only inside the Archive.

Historical locations remain historically believable.

---

## Pillar 2

The Archive never changes.

Historical eras constantly change.

The Archive remains visually consistent.

---

## Pillar 3

Characters are symbolic.

Every recurring character represents an idea.

Orphea

→ Guidance

Nephia

→ Compassion

Plague Doctor

→ Creation and Sacrifice

---

## Pillar 4

Beauty exists inside decay.

Nothing should feel clean.

Nothing should feel futuristic.

Everything carries age.

---

## Pillar 5

Silence is stronger than spectacle.

Large explosions.

Huge magical effects.

Overly dramatic action.

These are avoided whenever possible.

Tension should come from atmosphere.

---

# 4. Art Direction

## Official Style

**Cinematic Stylized Realism**

The project aims for believable realism enhanced by painterly composition and controlled stylization.

Characters should resemble real people while preserving iconic silhouettes.

---

## Secondary Influences

Dark Academia

Techno Gothic

Gaslamp Fantasy

Victorian Illustration

Historical Oil Painting

Gothic Architecture

Mechanical Astronomy

Museum Illustration

# 9. The Archive Principle

The Archive is outside history.

It is never affected by historical events.

Wars end.

Kingdoms fall.

Empires disappear.

The Archive remains unchanged.

---

# 10. Historical Principle

Every historical episode follows one rule.

> **History changes.**
>
> **The Archive interprets.**
>
> **It never rewrites.**

Historical authenticity is respected whenever possible.

Creative interpretation is allowed only to increase emotional understanding—not to distort documented history.

---

# 11. Production Rules

Before any image is approved, ask:

* Does this still feel like **Orphea Archive**?
* Does this respect the historical setting?
* Does every major visual element have narrative purpose?
* Could this image belong to another fantasy project? If yes, redesign it.
* Is the atmosphere restrained rather than exaggerated?
* Would this still be recognizable without dialogue?

If any answer is "No," revise the design before approval.

---

# 12. Studio Motto

> **Every era changes.**
>
> **The Archive never does.**

이 Part 1은 **세계관 설정집**이 아니라 **아트 디렉션 헌장**이야. 앞으로 새로운 시대(로마, 조선, 타이타닉, 체르노빌 등)를 추가할 때도 이 문서만 보면 "이게 Orphea Archive다운가?"를 판단할 수 있는 기준이 되도록 구성했어.
